What I Do

My passions lie in the theology, philosophy, and in the practice of visual art. I'm currently working on my first major art project: illustrating a children's book. I'm also studying Russian academic art with New Master's Academy, and I write about art and aesthetics on Substack.

My Philosophy

I believe that men create because we are made in the image of the Creator. We also have the privilege of pariticpating in the creative work of redeeming this fallen world by participating in the re-creative work of the Word Incarnate. All the forms of the natural world are manifestations of the Divine Mind, and we artists strive to imitate God by materializing the concepts in our own minds.
